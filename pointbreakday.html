Thrill seekers and adventurers wanted!

TheCaliCamp has just what you’re looking for. We are introducing “The Point Break Day”.

Here’s what you can expect: SURFING and SKYDIVING
  • 8 am Pickup at the camp
  • Southbound to San Diego looking for the best waves
  • 2 hour surf session
  • Lunch
  • 2:30 PM SKYDIVING (Views of Downtown San Diego, the ocean, into Mexico and beyond)
  • After a 13,000 ft freefall, you’ll be as full of adrenaline as you ever may be in your life.
  • 7 PM Beachside bonfire and BBQ
  • Let the adrenaline come down while sharing stories, food and drinks with the other guests

Skydiving is done at one of the world’s leading skydive schools: Skydive San Diego $169 paid direct to school
