This is about B-Kite

Forgot how to control the kite? Never tried it? No worries, we're nearby. Established in 2021, Bkite Zanzibar has swiftly progressed from island exploration in an old van to Paje Beach's newest kitesurfing school.

Located by Hakuna Majiwe, Bkite offers top-notch kite experiences. Private lessons, rentals, and more.

Best kitesurfing December–March and June–October. Visit and feel the difference!
