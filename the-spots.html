The Surf Spots

Kiteboarding in Zanzibar has become a top world class destination. The wind, the water, the weather makes an island an ideal place for the complete beginners needing a stress-free experience, as well as independent riders looking for untapped spot to session all day long. You will be amazed by possibilities that an island got from the mother nature: shallow lagoon, flat turquoise water and endless beaches with smooth white sand make this island a kiteboarders paradise. Barely 1.5km away from the shore you will find the reef where waves can reach up to 3 meters height. Perfect treat for waves lovers.

There are two wind seasons:

From mid-May to October the wind called Kusi blows from South to North coast with average wind speed between 12-19 knots. Constant side-on-shore wind from morning to late evening. (The average water temperature is between 24-28 degrees)

From mid-December to mid-March the wind called Kaskazi blows from North to South coast with average wind speed between 12-17 knots. Perfect side-on-shore wind conditions almost
